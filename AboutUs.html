About
i-Caffeine

Established in 2021, i-Caffeine is a website that can help people specifically people who passionate with coffee and the architecture of a cafe. As our name, we deliver information about cafes around Malang City. The information include name of cafe, menu and price, location, and rating. This will help people to get the best experience when drinking coffee.

We also collaborate with owner of the cafes around Malang City so that we always deliver updated information about cafe. From our website, we hope that drink coffee culture will continue increase and help local coffeeshop owner to get their profit.
